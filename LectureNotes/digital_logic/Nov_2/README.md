### Signed Extention Explained
   - 4-bit encoding of 6: 0110 
   - 8-bit encoding of 6: "0"000 0110
   - 16-bit encoding of 6: 0000 0000 0000 0110

   - 8-bit encoding of -6: "1"111 1010
   - 16-bit encoding of -6: 1111 1111 1111 1010
